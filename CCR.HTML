The Centre for Coastal Research is dedicated to preserving and restoring Ghana's coastal ecosystems through science, community action, and sustainable solutions. As an initiative under the Clean Ocean Project Ghana, we are committed to a future where our shores are resilient, vibrant, and thriving.

ABOUT US

At the Centre for Coastal Research, we believe the coast is more than a boundary‚Äîit's a lifeline. Our work is anchored in the intersection of marine science, environmental stewardship, and community resilience. We study coastal dynamics, erosion, pollution, and biodiversity to inform actions that protect both people and planet. Guided by evidence and empathy, we turn research into real-world impact along Ghana's precious shoreline.

Our Mission

To advance the understanding, protection, and sustainable management of Ghana's coastal and marine environments through interdisciplinary research, innovative solutions, and inclusive community engagement.
